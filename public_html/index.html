About Me

I'm a computer engineering graduate from the University at Buffalo. I'm currently working with distributed and networked systems. My interest lies in implementing robust, transparent and well-designed systems that are resilient to failure using the relevant languages. I'm AZ-900 certified, and highly motivated and passionate about working on cloud technologies.

In my time at Shweta, our team built Android applications via Flutter as well as some Python webscraping/ETL tools and pipelines, with me serving as a software developer and liason with clients. I have a strong grasp of databases and have a high proficiency at debugging in general. What I enjoy most about the computer engineering field is our ability to attack and solve complex problems, and to wear many hats and be versatile.

I'm a fast learner and always open to new technologies that help improve the workflow of the projects I work on, and with the ability to hit the ground running with fast-paced work environments. I have a good sense of teamwork, and I'm always eager to learn and ask questions.
