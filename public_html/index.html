About Me

I'm a computer engineering graduate from the University at Buffalo. I'm currently working with distributed and networked systems. My interest lies in implementing robust, transparent and well-designed systems that are resilient to failure using the relevant languages. I'm AZ-900 certified, and highly motivated and passionate about working on cloud technologies.

As a software engineer at Packmoose LLC, I worked on various software engineering, data engineering and analytics projects, getting extremely familiar with AWS components pertaining to the same, from Amplify and API gateway for web components to S3, Athena, Glue and DynamoDB for various data engineering tasks. I also worked on code reviews on large SQL queries, figuring out ways to make them readable and clear technical debt.

In my time at Shweta, our team built Android applications via Flutter as well as some Python webscraping/ETL tools and pipelines, with me serving as a software developer and liason with clients. What I enjoy most about the computer engineering field is our ability to attack and solve complex problems, and to wear many hats and be versatile.

I'm a fast learner and always open to new technologies that help improve the workflow of the projects I work on, and with the ability to hit the ground running with fast-paced work environments. I have a good sense of teamwork, and I'm always eager to learn and ask questions.
